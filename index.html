About Us

Our Mission

At Berimbolo Security, we are dedicated to providing the highest level of security services to protect what matters most to you. Our mission is to deliver innovative and effective security solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Our History

Founded in 1998, Berimbolo Security has grown from a small local business to a leading provider of security services in the region. With over two decades of experience, we have developed a reputation for excellence and reliability.

Our Values

We pride ourselves on our core values of integrity, professionalism, and commitment to customer service. These principles guide every aspect of our business and ensure that we meet the highest standards of ethical conduct and quality service.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive security solutions including:

  1. Security risk assessments
  2. Sale and installation of alarm systems and CCTV
  3. Security system maintenance
  4. 24/7 monitoring of alarm systems and CCTV footage
